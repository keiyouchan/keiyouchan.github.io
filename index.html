About Me

Hi! I am Jingrong, an upcoming PhD student at the Carbon Neutrality and Climate Change (CNCC) Thrust of the HKUST(GZ) and Research Assistant to John Van Reenen (LSE, MIT) and Robin Burgess (LSE).

My research interests sit at the intersection of Energy Economics and Environmental Economics. I am currently interested in how climate policies and energy market reforms shape firm behavior, industrial structure, and technological innovation.

Prior to HKUST(GZ), I completed M.A. in Industrial Economics at the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (SWUFE) and my B.A. in Economics at Huaqiao University.
